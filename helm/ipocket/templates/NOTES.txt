1. Access the application inside the cluster:

  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "ipocket.fullname" . }} {{ .Values.service.port }}:{{ .Values.service.port }}

2. Open in browser:

  http://127.0.0.1:{{ .Values.service.port }}/ui/login

3. Optional endpoints:

  http://127.0.0.1:{{ .Values.service.port }}/health
  http://127.0.0.1:{{ .Values.service.port }}/metrics
  http://127.0.0.1:{{ .Values.service.port }}/sd/node
